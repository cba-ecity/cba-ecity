About

Welcome to CBA!

The Ministry of CBA started in 2013 with a vision to establish a Brethren Assembly in Electronics City, Bangalore.

We started with a humble beginning and gradually consolidated ourselves as an autonomous Brethren Assembly on September 16th, 2018.

CBA is now a family of believers who love the Lord Jesus Christ and yearns to see His name be glorified in every aspect of our lives.

The Holy Bible is the finally authority for our faith and practice. Our desire continues to be to provide a church that is Christ-centered and to establish a community that cares for and serves people. We are committed to peace and to ardently follow Jesus Christ to:

  1. Grow Spiritually
  2. Build a relationship with one another and 3) Share the good news of the Gospel.Â 

The various activities of the church include, but are not limited to:

  1. Visions India Counselling Centre
  2. Media/Publication
  3. Helping Hand Ministry

If you are looking for a church where you would feel accepted and be taught in-depth from the Word of God, hesitate not to join us.

When you are in Bangalore, we invite you to come and worship with us!
