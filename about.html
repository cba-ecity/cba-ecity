About

In March 2009, Bro. John T Thomas, who is a member of the Keekozhoor Brethren Assembly (Kerala), devoted himself to the Lord’s ministry. Later in 2013, in order to continue his biblical studies, the church sent him to Bangalore. After completing his post graduation course, he and his family were involved with spreading the word of God in Electronic city, Bangalore where there are only a few worship gatherings.

The Lord blessed Bro. John's labour in the field. By God’s grace, on 16 September 2018, in the presence of members of the Madiwala assembly, Keekozhoor Brethren Assembly, and other brothers and sisters in Christ, the first gathering of the Christian Believers Assembly - Electronics City began with thanksgiving and offering up the church to God.

The church gathers for worship every Sunday, from 9.30am to 11.30am, in Lalith building which is near the Suraksha Motors building in Electronic city.

This church will be a blessing to the people who come to Bangalore in relation to their studies and jobs. Kindly uphold this assembly, as well as the other ministries in Electronic city, in your prayers.
